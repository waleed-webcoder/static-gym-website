WHAT IS BMR?

BMR, or Basal Metabolic Rate, represents the number of calories your body needs to perform basic functions while at rest.
These functions include

  • i- breathing,
  • ii- circulating blood
  • iii- maintaining body temperature
  • iv- metabolism
  • v- cell function, growth, and repair
